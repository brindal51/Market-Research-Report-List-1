<p><p>The audio drivers market consists of various types, each designed to reproduce specific frequency ranges. Tweeters handle high frequencies for clarity in vocals and instruments, while midrange drivers produce a balanced sound for vocals and instruments alike. Woofers focus on low frequencies, delivering deep bass. Subwoofers enhance the low end even further, providing power and impact. Super tweeters extend high-frequency performance. Crossovers are essential components that divide audio signals among these drivers, ensuring optimal sound quality and performance in audio systems.</p></p>
