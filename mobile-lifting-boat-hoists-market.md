<p><p>Mobile lifting boat hoists are categorized into two main types: self-propelled boat hoists and towed boat hoists. Self-propelled boat hoists are equipped with their own propulsion systems, allowing for easy maneuvering and positioning at marinas or docks without additional vehicles. In contrast, towed boat hoists rely on external vehicles for transportation, making them more versatile for long distances but less agile at docks. Both types enhance efficiency in launching and retrieving boats, catering to diverse marine operations.</p></p>
