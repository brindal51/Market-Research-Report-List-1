<p><ul><li>Digital IO Cards</li><li>Analog IO Cards</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The IO cards market encompasses two main types: digital IO cards and analog IO cards. Digital IO cards handle binary signals, enabling devices to detect on/off states and control systems in applications like automation and data acquisition. Conversely, analog IO cards process continuous signals, facilitating the measurement and control of variables such as temperature, pressure, and voltage. Both types are essential for interfacing with sensors and actuators, driving performance across various industries, including manufacturing, healthcare, and automotive.</p></p>
