<p><strong>The Polypectomy Forcep Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Hot</li><li>Cold</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The polypectomy forceps market is divided into two primary types: hot and cold. Hot polypectomy forceps utilize electrocautery to remove polyps, allowing for simultaneous cutting and coagulation to minimize bleeding. In contrast, cold polypectomy forceps operate at room temperature, mechanically excising polyps without thermal energy, which can be beneficial in specific clinical scenarios. Each type caters to different medical needs, providing healthcare professionals with options based on the patient's condition and the nature of the polyps being removed.</p></p>
