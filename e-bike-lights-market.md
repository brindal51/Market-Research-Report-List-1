<p><p>The E-bike lights market is primarily divided into two categories: front lights and rear lights. Front lights enhance visibility and safety by illuminating the path ahead and making cyclists more visible to motorists. They often come with various brightness levels and features like adjustable beams. Rear lights, on the other hand, are designed to signal a cyclist's presence to traffic behind, typically featuring flashing or steady light modes. Both types are essential for safe riding conditions, particularly in low-light environments.</p></p>
