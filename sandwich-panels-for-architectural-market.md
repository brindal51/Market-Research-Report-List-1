<p><p>Sandwich panels are composite building materials used in the architectural market, combining insulation and structural support. PUR/PIR sandwich panels feature polyisocyanurate or polyurethane cores, providing excellent thermal insulation and fire resistance. EPS sandwich panels utilize expanded polystyrene cores, offering cost-effective insulation but with lower fire resistance. Mineral wool sandwich panels use rock or glass wool, delivering superior fire protection and sound insulation. Other market types may include bio-based materials or specialized composites designed for specific architectural applications, enhancing sustainability and performance.</p></p>
