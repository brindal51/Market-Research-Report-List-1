<p><strong>The Water TOC Testing Instrument Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>NDIR Method</li><li>UV Method</li><li>Conductance Method</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Water Total Organic Carbon (TOC) testing instruments are classified into several methods based on their detection techniques. The NDIR (Non-Dispersive Infrared) method measures CO2 produced from organic compounds, offering high sensitivity. The UV method detects organic carbon through absorbance of ultraviolet light, suitable for low-concentration samples. The conductance method assesses conductivity changes during organic matter oxidation, providing a quick assessment. Other methods may include techniques like chromatography or advanced oxidation processes, expanding the versatility of TOC measurement in water analysis.</p></p>
