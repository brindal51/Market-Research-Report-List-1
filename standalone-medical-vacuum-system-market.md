<p><strong>The Standalone Medical Vacuum System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Dry Claw Pump Techology</li><li>Dry Rotary Vane Technology</li><li>Oil Sealed Rotary Vane Technology</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Standalone Medical Vacuum System market consists of three primary types: Dry Claw Pump Technology, which uses two claw-shaped rotors to create a vacuum without oil, reducing contamination risk; Dry Rotary Vane Technology, which employs rotating vanes within a chamber, allowing for a high level of efficiency and minimal maintenance; and Oil Sealed Rotary Vane Technology, which utilizes oil for lubrication to enhance performance, though it may carry a higher risk of oil contamination, thus requiring careful management in medical environments.</p></p>
