<p><p>The pharmaceutical amino acids market is divided into two main categories: non-essential amino acids and essential amino acids. Non-essential amino acids, such as alanine and glutamic acid, can be synthesized by the body and play roles in metabolic processes and neurotransmission. Essential amino acids, like lysine and leucine, must be obtained through diet as the body cannot produce them. Both types are crucial in drug formulation, nutritional supplements, and therapeutic applications, catering to various health and dietary needs.</p></p>
