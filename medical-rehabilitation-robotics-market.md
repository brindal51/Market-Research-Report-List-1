<p><p>The Medical Rehabilitation Robotics market can be categorized into upper extremity and lower extremity segments. Upper extremity rehabilitation robots focus on assisting patients with arm and hand movement recovery, targeting conditions like stroke or orthopedic injuries. These devices enhance motor skills and dexterity through repetitive, guided exercises. Lower extremity rehabilitation robots, on the other hand, are designed for patients recovering from leg-related issues, including spinal cord injuries and post-surgery rehabilitation, facilitating gait training and mobility improvement.</p></p>
