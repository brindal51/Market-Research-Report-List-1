<p><p>Poppet solenoid valves are essential components used to control the flow of fluids in various applications. The market is segmented into normally-closed and normally-open types. Normally-closed valves remain shut when not energized, allowing fluid flow only when actuated. This design is ideal for safety-critical applications. Conversely, normally-open valves allow fluid flow in their default state and close when energized, suitable for systems requiring flow interruption. Each type serves distinct operational needs in industries such as automation, HVAC, and fluid management.</p></p>
